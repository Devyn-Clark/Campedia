Backpack

What to look for in a purchase

  • When purchasing a backpack for your camping trip, the amount of things your pack can hold is an obvious key issue. Most backpacks have their capacity measured by way of liters; they can range from 35 to 75 liters. How large a pack you should purchase is dependent on how long your trip will be. The materials the bag is made of is also important. An ideal pack would need a strong strength to weight ratio. Popular materials would include, nylon, polyester, polypropylene, and canvas.

Common Backpack Mistakes

  • The most common mistake for backpacks is caring too much in your bag. If you need more containers, then do so at the discrection of your budget.

  • Another mistake is overlooking the features a certain backpack can offer: extra pockets, Gear Loops, and a Hydration Bladder in favor of a simplier pack.

Using backpacks effectively

  • Plan ahead and base the pack you choose based on the objects you pln to bring with you.
